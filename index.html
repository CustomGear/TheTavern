Outdoor Patio — Weather Dependent

The Tavern Group operates seasonal outdoor patios across four Ottawa locations. All patios are weather-dependent and may close or adjust hours during rain, storms, or extreme temperatures. We recommend checking our social channels or calling your location before visiting. Normal hours resume as soon as skies clear.

Our Story

Ottawa's Outdoor
Patio Since Day One

The Tavern Group was born from a simple idea: Ottawa deserved outdoor gathering spaces as beautiful as its skyline. We set out to build patios with soul — places where the view is as good as the drink in your hand.

From our flagship location beside the National Gallery to our serene Island perch overlooking Gatineau Hills, every Tavern location is designed to make you feel like you're somewhere special — because you are.
